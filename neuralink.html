Neuralink



Background Information

Neuralink is a brain implant that is currently being developed by Elon Musk. It is designed to let an individual control a computer or mobile device no matter where you go. The implant is installed via a surgical robot that inserts ultra-thin threads with a needle. The implant is surrounded in a biocompatible shell that protects the implant from outside hazards. Additionally, the implant features a battery that can be wirelessly charged with a compact charger. Furthermore, the implant contains chips and electronics designed to transmit data.
