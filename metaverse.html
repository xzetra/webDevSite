Metaverse



Background Information

The Metaverse launched after Facebook rebraned to "Meta" in 2021. The Metaverse is a virtual world being developed by Meta, which was created by Mark Zuckerberg. According to Mark, this virtual world will enable individuals to connect with each other and share experiences together. The metaverse can currently be accessed via VR headset, augmented reality, or smart glasses. The Metaverse is not just a virtual world but a place where ideas come to life.
